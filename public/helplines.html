🛡️ Safety Tips

1️⃣
Stay calm — Take a deep breath. Panic makes it harder to think clearly.
2️⃣
Press SOS — Use SafeAlert SOS button to alert your emergency contacts instantly.
3️⃣
Move to a crowded place — Go to a shop or any public area with people around.
4️⃣
Call 112 — India's single emergency number for police, ambulance and fire.
5️⃣
Make noise — Shout for help, use a whistle or honk if you're in a vehicle.
