ℹ️ How Safe Walk works

1
Set your duration — Choose how long your walk will take (15–60 mins)
2
Start walking — Your emergency contacts are notified that you started a safe walk
3
Check in regularly — Press "I'm Safe" at each check-in interval to confirm you're okay
4
Auto SOS if you miss — If you miss a check-in, an SOS alert is automatically sent with your location
5
Stop when done — Press Stop when you've safely reached your destination
